Travel

The Invasion Group is a collection of specialist brands, each dedicated to delivering once-in-a-lifetime holiday experiences.
Through working in partnership with over 100+ university societies, Invasion’s Weekend Breaks team have helped more than 6000 students in their journey to Europe and back – and that’s just in the last six months! With partners based in Berlin, Budapest, Prague and of course our flagship destination, Amsterdam, Invasion offer only the very best weekend packages, all at an affordable and competitive price. This typically includes:
– 2+ nights hostel accommodation in your chosen city
– Executive Return travel (coach/flights depending on destination)
– Personable, professional Invasion representatives
– ABTA bonded/ATOL covered travel
– Complimentary promotional materials
Personal travel insurance and destination-specific extras are also available as an optional add-on, such as exclusive club nights, boat parties and of course the renowned Invasion Bar Crawl amongst much more!
Invasion trips run all-year round, so if you’re part of a university society or group, it’s not too late to get in touch about how we can help you to organise your society’s greatest trip yet!
